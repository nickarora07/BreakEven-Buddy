Calculation Logic

  • Delivered Orders: Math.floor(Orders × DeliveryRate/100)
  • RTO Orders: Orders − DeliveredOrders
  • Gross Revenue (incl GST): DeliveredOrders × AOV
  • Output GST: GrossRevenue × OutputGSTRate/100
  • Net Revenue (excl GST): GrossRevenue − OutputGST
  • Total Product Cost: DeliveredOrders × ProductCost
  • Total Shipping Cost: DeliveredOrders × ShippingCost
  • Total RTO Cost: RTOOrders × RTOCost
  • Total CPA Cost: Orders × CPA
  • Input GST Credit: (TotalProductCost × InputGSTRate/100) + (TotalShippingCost × 0.18) + (TotalCPACost × 0.18)
  • GST Payable: OutputGST − InputGSTCredit
  • Total Expenses: ProductCost + ShippingCost + RTOCost + CPACost + GSTPayable
  • Net Profit: NetRevenue − TotalExpenses
  • Profit per Delivered Order: NetProfit / DeliveredOrders
